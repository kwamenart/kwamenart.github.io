ABOUT ME

KWAME
NART

Acoustic Singer • Guitarist • Songwriter

Hi, I'm Kwame Nart, a passionate acoustic singer-guitarist based near Peterborough. I perform crowd-pleasing classics, modern favourites, and original music.

My Style Includes:

  • Soul & Pop
  • Folk & Country
  • Reggae & Feel-Good Classics
  • Original Songs

Whether it's a pub, wedding, festival or corporate event, I deliver engaging live performances that connect with all ages. I perform across Cambridgeshire and Lincolnshire and can bring my own professional PA system.
